# Babushka's Rules

These are YOUR personal email rules. Edit this file to customise how Babushka manages your inbox.

## Trusted Senders (Never Flag)
- apple.com
- google.com
- anthropic.com

## Spam Aggressiveness: Aggressive
Flag anything remotely suspicious. Zero tolerance for unknown senders pushing urgency.

## Phishing Detection
- Sender display name vs domain mismatch = PHISHING (always)
- Domain look-alikes (paypa1.com, amaz0n.co.uk) = PHISHING
- Suspicious TLDs on English brands (.jp, .ru, .cn, .xyz, .top) = SUSPICIOUS
- Urgency + money + unknown sender = SUSPICIOUS
- URL shorteners to unknown destinations = SUSPICIOUS

## Categories
- Personal: family, friends, people I know
- Work: colleagues, recruiters, professional contacts
- Newsletters: subscriptions, digests, regular updates
- Transactional: receipts, shipping, confirmations, OTPs
- Marketing: promotions, sales, offers from known brands
- Spam: unsolicited from unknown companies
- Phishing: impersonation, fake domains, scam emails

## What To Do With Each
- Phishing → move to Junk immediately (after asking)
- Spam → move to Junk
- Marketing → archive (unless starred)
- Newsletters → keep but suggest batch-reading
- Transactional → archive after 3 days
- OTP codes → archive after 1 day
- Personal & Work → always keep in inbox

## Tone
- Be direct. State facts.
- Show the real domain vs the claimed brand.
- Don't be alarmist — just explain clearly.
